About TECHKIE VISUALS

Where creativity meets technical excellence — a Derby-based creative and IT consultancy company.

Who We Are

TECHKIE VISUALS LIMITED is a UK-based creative and technical services company specialising in video production, post-production, IT consultancy, and professional photography. Established in 2024 and registered in England and Wales (Company No. 15919264), we serve businesses, organisations, and individuals throughout the United Kingdom and beyond.

Our headquarters in Derby, England, serves as the creative hub where innovative ideas transform into compelling visual content and robust technical solutions. We combine artistic vision with technical precision to deliver exceptional results for our clients.

Our Expertise

With comprehensive capabilities across video production, post-production, information technology consultancy, and portrait photography, we offer integrated solutions that address the full spectrum of our clients' creative and technical needs.

Our video production services encompass concept development, filming, and complete project management. Our post-production team brings expertise in editing, colour grading, motion graphics, and visual effects. Our IT consultancy services help businesses optimise their technology infrastructure and digital operations. Our photography services capture professional portraits and commercial imagery that resonates with audiences.

Our Approach

We believe in collaborative partnerships with our clients. Every project begins with understanding your goals, audience, and vision. We then apply our technical expertise and creative innovation to deliver solutions that exceed expectations.

Quality, professionalism, and client satisfaction drive everything we do. Whether you need a corporate video, IT infrastructure assessment, or professional photography, we approach each project with the same commitment to excellence.

Why Choose Us

Our unique combination of creative services and technical consultancy allows us to serve as a comprehensive partner for organisations seeking both visual storytelling and technology solutions. We understand that today's businesses require both compelling content and robust technical infrastructure.

As a registered UK company, we operate with full transparency, professionalism, and accountability. Our clients trust us to deliver quality work on time and within budget.
